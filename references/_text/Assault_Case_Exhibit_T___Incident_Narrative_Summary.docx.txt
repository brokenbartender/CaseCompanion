Exhibit T — Incident Narrative Summary
Cody E. McKenzie — Assault on August 20, 2025 at Liberty Bar
Overview:
This exhibit provides a clear, chronological narrative of the assault that occurred on August 20, 2025 at Liberty Bar, the actions taken by the victims, the behavior of the aggressor, and the immediate aftermath. This summary is based on video evidence, witness accounts, and the police report.
Incident Summary:
On August 20, 2025, during his scheduled shift at Liberty Bar, Cody McKenzie intervened to protect a female patron, Shelby Herron, after she was assaulted by an intoxicated male customer. The aggressor had thrown a drink on Shelby and, according to witness statements and video evidence, struck her in the face. Cody restrained the aggressor to prevent further harm while waiting for police to arrive.
Sequence of Events:
1. Initial Assault on Shelby
• The aggressor threw a drink on Shelby.
• He admitted this on video, stating: “She was being so mean to me… that’s why I threw my drink on her.”
• Shelby displayed visible signs of distress and attempted to distance herself from the aggressor.
2. Cody Intervenes
• Cody stepped in to protect Shelby and prevent further violence.
• The aggressor continued escalating, prompting Cody to restrain him on the ground in a controlled manner.
• Cody informed the aggressor that police were on the way and repeatedly instructed him to stop resisting.
3. Aggressor’s Admission and Escalation
• While restrained, the aggressor justified his actions and never denied punching Shelby.
• He became increasingly agitated, attempting to break free.
• At Video 1 (02:22), he violently kicked backward into Cody, causing Cody to lose balance.
4. Break of Restraint
• In Video 2, the aggressor continued kicking and twisting until he dislodged Cody and stood up.
• Instead of retreating, he turned back toward Cody and Shelby, yelling profanity and posturing aggressively.
• Cody shouted for bystanders not to let him leave.
5. Shelby Secures a Witness
• Shelby asked a nearby patron: “I need you to watch this, please.”
• This ensured third‑party corroboration of the aggressor’s behavior.
6. Police Arrival
• Officers arrived moments after the restraint broke.
• The aggressor was still agitated and yelling.
• Cody and Shelby provided statements consistent with the video evidence.
Victim Impact:
• Cody sustained shoulder and torso injuries from restraining the aggressor and from being kicked.
• Shelby experienced physical and emotional harm from the initial assault.
• Both victims displayed credible, consistent behavior throughout the incident.
Aggressor Behavior Summary:
• Intoxicated, erratic, and escalating.
• Retaliatory mindset, believing Shelby’s “rudeness” justified violence.
• Admitted to battery on video.
• Actively resisted restraint and assaulted Cody.
• Refused to retreat even when standing beside an exit.
Conclusion:
The incident on August 20, 2025 involved a clear and unprovoked assault on Shelby Herron, followed by an assault on Cody McKenzie during his attempt to protect her and prevent further harm. The aggressor’s actions, admissions, and continued escalation are fully documented in the video evidence. Cody’s actions remained controlled, defensive, and consistent with ensuring safety until police arrived.
