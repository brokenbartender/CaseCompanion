McKenzie Defendant: Jeffery Snyder Incident Date: August 20, 2025 Location: Liberty Bar &amp; Poutinerie, 85 N Saginaw St, Pontiac, MI Law Enforcement Case #: OCSO 25‑195158 Criminal Case #: 2025‑251105SM‑SM    INCIDENT OVERVIEW   On August 20, 2025, while working as a bartender at Liberty Bar &amp; Poutinerie, I was the victim of a violent, unprovoked assault committed by customer  Jeffery Snyder . The incident began inside the bar and continued into the hallway, where additional witnesses observed his aggressive behavior. He also struck a female patron and attempted to hit additional individuals during the escalation.     As the situation moved toward the hallway, Snyder continued acting aggressively until he was physically restrained by bystanders.     The hallway portion of the incident was recorded by witness  Wrobel , capturing Snyder’s continued aggression and the aftermath of the initial assault.     Although the initial punch is not on video, the recorded footage documents Snyder’s ongoing violent behavior consistent with the injuries sustained.   INJURIES &amp; MEDICAL TREATMENT     I experienced immediate pain, weakness, and limited range of motion in my right shoulder.     I sought emergency medical treatment at  Trinity Health , where imaging and examination confirmed a shoulder injury consistent with blunt‑force trauma.     Treatment included diagnostic imaging, pain management, and follow‑up care.     I missed  8 days of work  due to the injury.     I incurred  no out‑of‑pocket medical expenses .      WITNESSES &amp; EVIDENCE    Witness:      Wrobel  — recorded video of the hallway portion of the incident and observed Snyder’s continued aggression.
