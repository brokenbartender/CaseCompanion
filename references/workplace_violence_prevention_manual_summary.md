# Workplace Violence Prevention: A Guide (MS AG) - Summary

Source: https://www.ojp.gov/pdffiles1/Digitization/210067NCJRS.pdf

What it is:
- A workplace violence prevention guide published by the Mississippi Attorney General's Office.
- Defines workplace violence types and provides prevention/response guidance.

Key items (high-level):
- Type II violence: committed against an employee by a client, customer, patient, or inmate; often nonfatal assaults or verbal threats.
- Emphasizes zero-tolerance policies and reporting protocols.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Supports a Workplace Violence context module and a Type II violence definition used in incident framing.
- Helps define an internal "Zero Tolerance" policy template and employer-liability discussion prompts.
