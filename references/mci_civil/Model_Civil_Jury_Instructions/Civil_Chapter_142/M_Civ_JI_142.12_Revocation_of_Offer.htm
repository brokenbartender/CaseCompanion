M Civ JI 142.12 Revocation of Offer

An offer may be revoked by the person who made it. It may be revoked for any reason or no reason. It does not need to be in writing. To be effective, the revocation must be communicated to the other party before the offer is accepted.

Comment

Board of Control of Eastern Michigan University v Burgess, 45 Mich App 183 (1973); Kutsche v Ford, 222 Mich 442 (1923).

History

M Civ JI 142.12 was added March 2005.
