M Civ JI 142.14 Time of Acceptance

An acceptance becomes effective when it is communicated to the person who made the offer. An offer that has been revoked or is no longer open cannot be accepted.

Comment

Kutsche v Ford, 222 Mich 442 (1923); Pakideh v Franklin Commercial Mortgage Group, 213 Mich App 636 (1995).

History

M Civ JI 142.14 was added March 2005.
