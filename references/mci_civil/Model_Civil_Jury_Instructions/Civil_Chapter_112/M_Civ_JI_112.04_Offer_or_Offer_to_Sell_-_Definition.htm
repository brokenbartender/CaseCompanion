M Civ JI 112.04 Offer or Offer to Sell - Definition

The terms “offer” or “offer to sell” include an attempt to offer to dispose of a franchise or interest in a franchise for value. It also includes solicitation of an offer to buy a franchise or interest in a franchise for value. It does not include the renewal or extension of an existing franchise where there is no interruption in the operation of the franchised business by the franchisee.

Comment

MCL 445.1503(3).

History

Added July 2017.
