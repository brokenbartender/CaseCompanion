M Civ JI 112.08 Payment for Goods and Services as Franchise Fee

If a franchisee is forced to pay a price in excess of a bona fide wholesale price for goods or if the franchisee is required to purchase excess goods for which there is no well-established market in this state, the excess costs borne by the franchisee in favor of the franchisor constitutes the payment of an indirect franchise fee.

Comment

Hamade v Sunoco, Inc, 271 Mich App 145, 157; 721 NW2d 233 (2006).

History

Added July 2017.
