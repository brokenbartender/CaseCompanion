M Civ JI 115.08 Assault and Battery - Defense - Right to Resist an Unlawful Arrest

A citizen has the right to resist an unlawful arrest. However, the amount of force a citizen may use to resist an unlawful arrest must be reasonable under the circumstances.

Comment

See People v Krum, 374 Mich 356; 132 NW2d 69 (1965).

History

M Civ JI 115.08 was added September 1982.
