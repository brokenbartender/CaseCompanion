M Civ JI 143.03 Sales Representative - Defined

The term “sales representative” means a person who contracts with or is employed by a principal for the solicitation of orders or sale of goods and is paid, in whole or in part, by commission. Sales representative does not include a person who places an order or sale for a product on his or her own account for resale by that sales representative.

Comment

MCL 600.2961(1)(e). “Person” is defined in MCL 600.2961(1)(b).

History

Adopted January 2020.
