M Civ JI 13.07 Attempted Rescue of One in Imminent Peril by a Person Who Did Not Cause Such Peril [ Instruction Deleted ]

Comment

Former M Civ JI 13.07 was deleted because the subject matter of that instruction is covered by the general negligence instructions. See Solomon v Shuell, 435 Mich 104; 457 NW2d 669 (1990).

History

M Civ JI 13.07 was added September 1980. Deleted February 1991.
