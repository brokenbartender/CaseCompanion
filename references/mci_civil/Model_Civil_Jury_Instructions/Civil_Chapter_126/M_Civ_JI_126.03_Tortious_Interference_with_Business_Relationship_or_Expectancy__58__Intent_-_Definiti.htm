M Civ JI 126.03 Tortious Interference with Business Relationship or Expectancy: Intent - Definition

When I say that plaintiff must prove that defendant intentionally interfered with the business relationship or expectancy, I mean that

(a) defendant’s primary, but not necessarily sole, purpose was to interfere with plaintiff’s business relationship or expectancy, or

(b) defendant acted knowing that [ his / her ] conduct was certain or substantially certain to cause interference with plaintiff’s business relationship or expectancy.
