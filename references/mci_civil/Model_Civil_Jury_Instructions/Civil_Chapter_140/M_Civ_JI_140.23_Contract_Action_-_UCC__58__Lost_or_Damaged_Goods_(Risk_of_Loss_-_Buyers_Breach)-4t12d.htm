M Civ JI 140.23 Contract Action - UCC: Lost or Damaged Goods (Risk of Loss - Buyer’s Breach)

If the buyer repudiated or otherwise breached the contract after existing conforming goods were shipped, marked, or otherwise designated to the contract, to the extent that the seller [ has not received / will not receive ] insurance proceeds, [ he / she / it ] may recover damages from the buyer if the goods were [ lost / damaged ] within a commercially reasonable time after the repudiation or other breach.

Comment

MCL 440.2510(3).

History

M Civ JI 140.23 was added January 1987.
