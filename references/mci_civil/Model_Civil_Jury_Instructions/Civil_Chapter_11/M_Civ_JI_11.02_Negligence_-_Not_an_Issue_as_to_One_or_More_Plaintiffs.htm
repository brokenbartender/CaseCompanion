M Civ JI 11.02 Negligence - Not an Issue as to One or More Plaintiffs

You must not consider whether there was negligence on the part of the [ plaintiff, [ name of plaintiff ] / plaintiffs, [ names of plaintiffs ] ], *(because [ explain briefly ]).

Note on Use

If there is a question for the jury as to the negligence of one or more plaintiffs, but not as to other plaintiffs, both M Civ JI 11.01 and M Civ JI 11.02 should be given.

*The words in parentheses may be added if appropriate.

History

M Civ JI 11.02 is a revision of SJI 11.02. Amended September 1980.
