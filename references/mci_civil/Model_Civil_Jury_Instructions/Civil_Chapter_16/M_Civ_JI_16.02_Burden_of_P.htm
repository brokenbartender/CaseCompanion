M Civ JI 16.02 Burden of Proof in Negligence Cases on the Issues and Legal Effect Thereof [INSTRUCTION DELETED]

Comment

This instruction was deleted because it applied solely to lawsuits filed on or before March 28, 1996, when the 1996 Tort Reform legislation took effect.

History

M Civ JI 16.02 is a revision of SJI 21.02. Amended September 1980. Deleted January 2020.
