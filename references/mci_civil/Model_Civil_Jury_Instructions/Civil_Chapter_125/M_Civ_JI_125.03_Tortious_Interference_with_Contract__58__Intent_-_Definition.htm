M Civ JI 125.03 Tortious Interference with Contract: Intent - Definition

When I say that plaintiff must prove that defendant intentionally interfered with the contract, I mean that

(a) defendant’s primary, but not necessarily sole, purpose was to cause [ name of breaching party ] to breach the contract, or

(b) defendant acted knowing that [ his / her ] conduct was certain or substantially certain to cause [ name of breaching party ] to breach the contract.
