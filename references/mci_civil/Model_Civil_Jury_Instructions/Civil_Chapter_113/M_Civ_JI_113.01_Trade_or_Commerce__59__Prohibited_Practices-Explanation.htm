M Civ JI 113.01 Trade or Commerce; Prohibited Practices-Explanation

We have a state law known as the Consumer Protection Act, which provides that certain unfair, unconscionable, or deceptive methods, acts, or practices in the conduct of trade or commerce are unlawful.

Note on Use

MCL 445.904 provides that the Act does not apply to certain regulated transactions or conduct and methods, acts, or practices already made unlawful by certain other statutes.

Comment

MCL 445.903.

History

Added July 2012.
