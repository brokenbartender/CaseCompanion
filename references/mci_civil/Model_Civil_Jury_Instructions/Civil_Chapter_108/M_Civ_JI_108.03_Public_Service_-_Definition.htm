M Civ JI 108.03 Public Service - Definition

When I use the term “public service” I mean a public facility, department, agency, board, or commission, owned, operated, or managed by or on behalf of this state, a political subdivision, or an agency of this state or of a political subdivision or a tax exempt private agency established to provide service to the public, except that public service does not include a state or county correctional facility with respect to actions and decisions regarding an individual serving a sentence of imprisonment.

Note on Use

The list of entities included as a public service should be tailored to the facts of the case.

Comment

MCL 37.2301(b), as amended by 2023 PA 6 2023 PA 31; 2023 PA 45.

History

Added December 2008, October 2023.
