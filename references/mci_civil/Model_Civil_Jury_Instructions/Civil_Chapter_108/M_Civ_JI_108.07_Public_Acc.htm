M Civ JI 108.07 Public Accommodation/Public Service Discrimination - Sexual Harassment - Explanation   

Discrimination based on sex includes sexual harassment. Sexual harassment is a type of sex discrimination prohibited by state law. There are two types of sexual harassment. The first is known as quid pro quo, which means “this for that.” The second is known as sexually hostile environment harassment. In this case plaintiff claims [ quid pro quo / sexually hostile environment ] harassment.
