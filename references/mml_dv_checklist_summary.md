# Michigan Municipal League DV Prosecution Checklist - Summary

Source: https://www.mml.org/pdf/mlc/dv-checklist.pdf

What it is:
- A municipal litigation checklist for prosecuting domestic violence ordinances.
- Includes step-by-step compliance items and statutory references.

Key themes (high-level):
- Ordinance alignment with MCL 750.81.
- Penalty thresholds that trigger state reporting.
- Importance of record checks for repeat offenders.
- Process and documentation checklist orientation.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Provides a checklist pattern for victim-rights compliance and deadline-driven workflows.
- Useful for structuring a “Victim Rights & Compliance” module format.
