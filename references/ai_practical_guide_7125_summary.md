# AI Tools for Lawyers - A Practical Guide (MS Bar) - Summary

Source: https://www.msbar.org/media/jgagwizj/ai-practical-guide-7125.pdf

What it is:
- A July 1, 2025 practical guide from the Mississippi Bar (Law Practice Management & Technology Committee).
- Summarizes benefits, risks, and categories of AI tools used by attorneys.

Key themes (high-level):
- Efficiency and document review gains.
- Research acceleration and drafting support.
- Cost savings and improved client service.
- Cautions around selection, governance, and responsible use.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Supports a user-facing "AI usage policy" section and guardrail disclosures.
- Helps define safe boundaries for AI assistance in a self-represented workflow.
