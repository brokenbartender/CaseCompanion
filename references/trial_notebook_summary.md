# The Trial Notebook (InfoQuest) - Summary

Source: https://www.ag.ky.gov/about/Office-Divisions/OCAHTPP/2020%20Child%20Abuse%20Prosecution%20Resources/The%20Trial%20Notebook.pdf

What it is:
- A practitioner-focused "trial notebook" guide with checklists and forms for trial preparation.
- Emphasizes organization, witness prep, and structured trial planning.

Key themes (high-level):
- Trial preparation checklists and materials organization.
- Witness and exhibit preparation routines.
- Case presentation and courtroom readiness.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Provides structure for a Trial Prep module (checklists, exhibit lists, witness sheets).
- Reinforces the need for pretrial organization views and printable trial packets.
