# Fact Pattern - Felonious Assault (Workplace)

Source basis: NELP U-Visa Practice Manual (Fact Pattern section, p. 7).

App-adapted wording (employee assaulted by client):
- Abusive touching, battery, beating, or use of a weapon by a client against an employee, resulting in substantial mental or physical harm.

Note:
- This is an adaptation of the original text to fit the employee‑victim/client‑assailant scenario.
