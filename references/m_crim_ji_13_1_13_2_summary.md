# M Crim JI 13.1 and 13.2 (Effective Nov 1, 2025) - Summary

Source: https://www.courts.michigan.gov/49f4bb/siteassets/rules-instructions-administrative-orders/jury-instructions/criminal/adopted/effnov2025/m-crim-ji-13.1-and-13.2-adopted-eff-nov-2025.pdf

What it is:
- Michigan Model Criminal Jury Instructions amendments for M Crim JI 13.1 and 13.2.
- Addresses assaulting/resisting/obstructing a police officer or person performing duties.

Key points (high-level):
- Emphasizes the need for jury instructions on the legal framework for whether officers’ actions were lawful.
- Removes “resisting/opposing” from M Crim JI 13.2 to align with MCL 750.479.
- Effective November 1, 2025.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Useful for understanding criminal-side proceedings and aligning civil narrative if officer‑related issues arise.
- Supports timeline accuracy where criminal case context references these instructions.
