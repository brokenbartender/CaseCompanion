# U Visas for Victims of Workplace Crime (NELP) - Summary

Source: https://www.nelp.org/app/uploads/2015/03/U-Visas-for-Victims-of-Workplace-Crime-Practice-Manual-NELP.pdf

What it is:
- A 2014 practice manual on U visas for immigrant victims of workplace crimes.
- Focuses on eligibility, certification, and process details.

Key themes (high-level):
- Eligibility requirements for U visas.
- Cooperation with law enforcement and certification.
- Documentation and evidence needed for applications.

Why it matters to CaseCompanion:
- Adds a victim‑support resources module for immigration‑related protections when workplace assault is involved.
- Provides a pathway to connect users with appropriate support if applicable.
